# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Cloud Security Engineers and security compliance teams responsible for discovering unauthorized (shadow) AI agents, evaluating risk profiles, and auditing access policies in Google Cloud Platform (GCP) environments.

## Product Purpose
To scan, inventory, and assess AI-enabled workloads (Shadow AI Agents) running inside GCP projects, providing security teams with clear visibility, risk scores, and architectural flow diagrams to secure AI usage.

## Brand Personality
- **Expert Confidence**: Highly secure, precise, and authoritative.
- **Minimalist & Restrained**: Focused on high utility and data density, avoiding unnecessary visual noise or decorative graphics.
- **Enterprise Professional**: A clean, balanced, and readable workspace tailored for security operators.

## Anti-references
- **Generic SaaS Templates**: Avoid generic "hero-metric" templates, overly thick side-stripe borders, or generic card grids.
- **Over-saturated Neon Glows**: Keep gradients and glowing shadows to a minimum; avoid making the app feel like a game dashboard.
- **Extreme Terminal Themes**: Avoid a raw "hacker" CLI look that sacrifices text readability or layout structure.

## Design Principles
- **Restrained Utility**: Visual design serves the data. Information density and readability are prioritized over decoration.
- **Transparent Heuristics**: Break down confidence and risk scores clearly, showing the specific security findings and env vars that triggered them.
- **Structural Integrity**: Maintain clean layouts, standard borders, and consistent spacing instead of rounded corners and excessive shadows.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **High-contrast Text**: Standard accessibility contrast matching WCAG AA guidelines for body text and labels.
- **Clear Visual Hierarchy**: Logical keyboard navigation order, focusable interactive elements, and semantic layouts.
- **Reduced Motion Support**: Fades and transitions respect the user's `@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)` settings.
